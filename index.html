About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering (DEIB) at Politecnico di Milano, where I am advised by Prof. Nicola Gatti. My current research focuses on online learning in constrained Markov decision processes. In particular, I'm interested in combining online learning theoretical tools with optimization techniques to build strategic agent able to act in complex constrained online environments.
