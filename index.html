About Me

I'm a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering (DEIB) at Politecnico di Milano, where I am advised by Prof. Nicola Gatti. During my Ph.D., I had the opportunity to be a visiting research at the Industrial Engineering & Operations Research Department (IEOR) at Columbia University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Christian Kroer. My current research focuses on online learning in constrained Markov decision processes. In particular, I'm interested in combining online learning theoretical tools with optimization techniques to build strategic agents able to act in complex constrained online environments.
