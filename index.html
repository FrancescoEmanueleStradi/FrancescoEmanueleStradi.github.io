About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Artificial Intelligence at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering (DEIB) at Politecnico di Milano, where I am advised by Prof. Nicola Gatti. During my Ph.D., I had the opportunity to be a visiting researcher at the Industrial Engineering & Operations Research (IEOR) Department at Columbia University, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Christian Kroer. My current research focuses on online learning in constrained Markov decision processes. In particular, I am interested in combining online learning theoretical tools with optimization techniques to build strategic agents able to act in complex constrained online environments.
